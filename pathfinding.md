# Act 1
## Underground Passage: Right 
* Take note of tile direction.  It's rather big: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b04a35sEH1o

## Forgotten Tower: Left

## Outer Cloister 
* Left waypoint: right
* Center cross with top right waypoint: straight
* Right waypoint with nothing in middle: left

## Jail 
* Level 1: Left for WP, straight for Lv 2
* Level 2: Straight for Lv 3
* Level 3: Left

## Catacombs 
* Level 2: Right from the waypoint


# Act 2
## Sewers
* Level 2: Left for WP, straight for Lv 3 (Lv 3 is left from WP)
* Level 3: Left

## Halls of the Dead: Left
* Lv 2: Straight for waypoint, left for Lv 3

## Maggot Lair: Right

## Claw Viper Temple: Left

## Tal Rasha's Tomb

# Act 3
## Flayer Dungeon
* Lv 1 & 2: Left

## Durance of Hate
* Lv 1: Left to lv 2
* Lv 2: 
  * Waypoint is left from entrance from lv 1.
  * Lv 3 is left from the waypoint (straight from entrance).

# Act 5
## Crystalline Passage, Glacial Trail, Ancient's Way
For icy cave tiles look at where the tile connection points to, not the "room" direction
* From Entrance:
  * Right: Waypoint
  * Left: Exit to Glacial Trail
  * Straight: Cave
* From the waypoint
  * Right: Cave
  * Left: Previous
  * Straight: Next
